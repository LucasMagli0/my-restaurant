Our Menu:


Pizza's

Margherita

A classic favorite featuring a thin crust topped with fresh tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves. Drizzled with extra virgin olive oil and sprinkled with a pinch of sea salt, this pizza embodies simplicity and authentic Italian flavors.

Quattro Formaggi

An indulgent blend of four cheeses: mozzarella, gorgonzola, fontina, and parmesan. This pizza offers a rich and creamy experience with a perfect balance of flavors, finished with a hint of garlic and fresh parsley.

Prosciutto e Rucola

A delicious combination of thinly sliced prosciutto and fresh arugula. The pizza is baked with mozzarella and then topped with the prosciutto and arugula post-baking, ensuring the greens remain crisp and the prosciutto tender. Finished with a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

Customizable Pizza

Create your own perfect pizza with our customizable option! Start with a classic thin crust base and choose from our rich tomato sauce or creamy white sauce. Then, select from our premium mozzarella, gorgonzola, fontina, parmesan, or dairy-free cheese.

    Toppings:

  • Meats: Pepperoni, Italian sausage, prosciutto, spicy salami, grilled chicken, anchovies, ham.
  • Vegetables: Bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, spinach, arugula, cherry tomatoes, black olives, artichoke hearts, zucchini, eggplant, roasted garlic.
  • Extras: Fresh basil, oregano, truffle oil, balsamic glaze, red chili flakes, capers, pine nuts.

Pasta's

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A simple yet flavorful dish made with spaghetti tossed in sautéed garlic, olive oil, and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes. Finished with fresh parsley and grated Parmesan, it’s a delightful, aromatic experience.

Penne Arrabbiata

This spicy pasta features penne coated in a robust tomato sauce infused with garlic and red chili peppers. The name "arrabbiata" means "angry," reflecting the heat from the peppers. It’s perfect for those who enjoy a kick!

Fettuccine Alfredo

A rich and creamy sauce made from butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese envelops fettuccine. This indulgent dish is a comforting favorite, often garnished with parsley and black pepper.

Make Your Own Pasta

Create your perfect pasta masterpiece! Choose your favorite shape, select a sauce base—like rich marinara or creamy Alfredo—then add your choice of protein, such as grilled chicken or shrimp. Toss in fresh vegetables and finish with herbs and cheese for a delightful, personalized meal. Enjoy a unique culinary experience tailored to your taste!

    Shapes:

  • Spaghetti, Penne, Fettuccine, Orecchiette, Farfalle

    Select a Sauce:

  • Tomato-based: Marinara, Arrabbiata, or Pomodoro
  • Cream-based: Alfredo, Carbonara, or Pesto
  • Oil-based: Aglio e Olio, Lemon Garlic, or Brown Butter Sage

    Additional Toppings:

  • Protien: Grilled chicken, sautéed shrimp, meatballs, or roasted vegetables.
  • Vegetables: Spinach, bell peppers, mushrooms, zucchini, or broccoli.
